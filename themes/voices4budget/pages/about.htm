Voices4Budget is a digital empowerment initiative supported by Accountability Lab, piloting in Nupabomba Village, Indonesia, and Ban Jamrung, Thailand. The program aims to enhance transparency and accountability in village fund management by providing accessible budget information, promoting community participation, strengthening oversight, and increasing digital literacy. Through these efforts, Voices4Budget seeks to ensure effective and equitable use of village funds, fostering sustainable development and improved quality of life for local communities.
